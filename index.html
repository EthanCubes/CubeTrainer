How do I use this program?

Tap and hold the screen or the spacebar until the timer text turns green, then let go to start the timer. Tap the spacebar or the screen again to stop the timer.

When you set the program to driller mode, you can choose from several sets of algorithms to train by timing them. It is advised to memorize the algorithms beforehand.

My cube doesn't look like the scramble net

Make sure that you are scrambling the cube with the white face on top and the green face facing towards you. If this still doesn't work then just do rescramble or something.
